Anti-Surveillance Viewpoint

Advantages

Prevent abuse of power

More privacy

Less likely to cause tension between nations

Disadvantages

Harder to prevent terrorism

Cant use tools to catch criminals

Conclusion

The constant abuse of power by the government attests that the tools cant be trusted by them and that having more law enforcement on U.S. soil should be enough to counteract the negatives of these not having these tools.
