Anti-Surveillance Viewpoint

Power Corrupts

Unlimited power leads to corruption

NSA(National Security Administration) has already been caught abusing their power multiple times

NSA Caught Spying on German Chancellor

Advantages

Prevent abuse of power

More privacy - less risk for identity theft

Less likely to cause tension between nations

Disadvantages

Harder to prevent terrorism

Cant use surveillance tools to catch criminals

Stakeholders

The United States government can't monitor all of its citizens and look into foreign threats

Citizens want to have their own privacy and not have all their conversations monitored and less likely to have their identity stolen

The rest of the world will want this because they will not be spied on and this will force mutual trust between nations

Surveillance development companies will lose a lot of business

Terrorists will have greater freedom to commit atrocities but alternative methods can be taken to prevent this

Conclusion

The constant abuse of power by the government attests that the tools cant be trusted by them and that having more law enforcement should be enough to counteract the negatives of not having these tools.
